Compress Image to Exact KB or MB (Free & Secure)

Welcome to ReduceSize.online, the most advanced tool designed to reduce image size in KB and MB without compromising quality. Whether you are a student applying for government jobs or a professional needing to optimize images for emails, our tool helps you compress JPG, PNG, and JPEG files to your exact required size (e.g., 20KB, 50KB, 100KB) instantly.

Why Do You Need Exact Size Compression?

Most online compressors only reduce quality by percentage, which makes it hard to hit a specific file size limit. However, ReduceSize allows you to input a specific number (like "50 KB"). Our algorithm automatically adjusts the quality and dimensions to match that target size perfectly. This is crucial for:

  • Government Exams: SSC, UPSC, IBPS, Railways, and NEET often require photos between 20KB to 50KB.
  • Admission Forms: Schools and colleges demand scanned signatures under 20KB.
  • Web Performance: Web developers use it to optimize site speed by reducing image load times.

How to Reduce Image Size for Online Forms?

  1. Upload: Click on the "Select File" button and choose your Image or PDF.
  2. Set Target: Enter your desired size (e.g., enter '50' for 50KB).
  3. Compress: Click "Compress & Download". The tool will process the file in milliseconds.
  4. Save: Your file is automatically downloaded, ready for upload.

Privacy First: We Do Not Store Your Photos

Security is our top priority. Unlike other server-side tools, ReduceSize.online works 100% in your browser. This means your personal photos, documents, and signatures never leave your device. No data is uploaded to any server, making it the safest way to compress sensitive documents.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I reduce image size to 20 KB exactly?
Yes! Just upload your photo, type "20" in the target size box, and our smart engine will compress it as close to 20 KB as possible without making it blurry.

Q: Does this tool support PDF compression?
Yes, we have a dedicated "PDF" tab where you can reduce the size of scanned PDF documents for easy sharing on WhatsApp or email.

Q: Is this service free?
Absolutely. You can compress unlimited images and PDFs for free. There are no hidden charges or sign-up requirements.

Q: Which formats are supported?
We support all major image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP, as well as standard PDF documents.
